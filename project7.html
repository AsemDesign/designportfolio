Branding for IKS Group

For IKS Group, I developed the full brand identity and website, creating a visual language that conveys strength, clarity, and reliability in the construction industry.

The logo was developed through an intensive hand-sketching process, using drawing as both a design method and reflective practice. The identity combines graphite and deep charcoal tones with a purposeful yellow accent—dark hues communicate stability, while yellow adds energy, contrast, and directional emphasis. Subtle textures reference construction materials, reinforcing the field without literal imagery.

The resulting brand system and website form a disciplined, coherent visual framework that balances rigor with approachability, positioning IKS Group as both dependable and forward-looking.
