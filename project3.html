Media Art Xploration

At MAX, a New York–based experimental media arts organization, I collaborated with the UX lead and designer to redesign the digital platform for donors, artists, and students. My work spanned research, analysis, and design exploration, balancing strategic thinking with hands-on implementation in WordPress.

Research & Discovery
Conducted competitive analysis, site audits, and content evaluation
Mapped user flows and early information architecture
Organized research and assets across Figma, Framer, Notion, and FigJam

Key Insights & Design Impact
What worked: Hero imagery and video conveyed MAX’s experimental tone, visual identity was strong, footer provided extra navigation context.
What needed improvement: Inconsistent hierarchy, disconnected components, confusing navigation, and limited accessibility.
Result:Redesigned site with clear structure, cohesive visuals, and scalable framework—modernizing the experience while supporting MAX’s mission to connect artists, audiences, and supporters.

Additional Contributions
Developed LinkedIn and Instagram content strategy, design assets, and posting plan
Grew Instagram following from 2,000 → 3,900 in a few months
