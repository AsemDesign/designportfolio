Brandbook design for Otbasy bank

I led the design and development of a comprehensive brand book for Otbasy Bank, one of Kazakhstan’s largest banks, known for mortgage services and its connection to families nationwide.

The visual system reflects the bank’s core values of trust, accessibility, and optimism, balancing professionalism with warmth through vibrant colors, thoughtful typography, and a flexible layout system. Designed for scalability, the brandbook supports regional branches with varying production budgets, offering cost-conscious yet visually strong solutions.

To enhance usability, I created a custom icon system for intuitive navigation and developed bilingual layouts—including dual-sided pamphlets—ensuring clarity and hierarchy in both languages.

The result is a cohesive, adaptable brand system that allows Otbasy Bank to maintain a friendly, family-oriented presence while communicating consistently at a national scale.
